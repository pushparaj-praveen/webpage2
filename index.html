How Block-UP Works

Our method is simple, yet comprehensive. To provide maximum security for your data and business operations, our system operates in four primary steps:

Monitoring

We perform constant vigilant monitoring for potential threats round the clock, to make sure that your data is always secure. Any perceived vulnerability is instantly identified and evaluated.

Investigation

Our dedicated security experts delve comprehensively into any potential threat in order to determine the best course of action and prevent any harm to your systems.

Incident Response

Upon identification of a threat, our rapid response mechanism kicks in. Depending on the nature of the threat, our advanced tools and expert analysts can respond both manually or automatically.

Remediation

We dont just deal with threats. Once a threat is neutralized, our team works on your system to prevent any future occurrences of a similar nature. We learn from each incident, and so does our system.
